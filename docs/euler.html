f(λ) is the orange curve, with the error bounds defined by the orange shaded region, with blue for g(λ) conversely. The white curve represents

$$\frac{\lambda}{\rho_{0}}+f(\lambda)+g(\lambda)$$

The intersection of the white curve with the real-axis gives the unstable real Eigen value to the linearized alpha-Euler equation.
