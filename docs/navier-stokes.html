f(λ) is the orange curve, with the error bounds defined by the orange shaded region, with blue for g(λ) conversely. The white curve represents

$$a_{0}(\lambda, \nu)+f(\lambda, \nu)+g(\lambda, \nu)$$

The intersection of the white curve with the real-axis gives the unstable real Eigen value to the linearized navier-stokes equation.
